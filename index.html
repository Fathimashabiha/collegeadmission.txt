About Us

Excellence to Significance

Chendamarai Ramanujam Educational Service Trust was founded in the year 2007 by Thiru C.Subramanian, an industrialist with a vision to empower the rural youth by imparting Technical Education.

In the year 2008 the institution began inducting students into Engineering College,approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University,Chennai.
